About CopAssist

Transforming law enforcement through AI-powered assistance

Our Story

CopAssist was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to create technology that helps law enforcement officers perform their duties more efficiently and safely, while improving community outcomes.

Founded in 2022 by a team of AI specialists and former law enforcement officers, we recognized the challenges faced by police departments across the country. Information overload, critical time constraints, and the need for split-second decision-making create enormous pressure on officers in the field.

Our AI-powered chatbot was designed to address these challenges by providing instant, relevant information and support to officers when they need it most. Today, CopAssist is used by over 200 law enforcement agencies nationwide, helping to protect both officers and the communities they serve.

🎯Our Mission

To empower law enforcement professionals with AI-driven solutions that enhance decision-making, improve safety, and strengthen community relationships.

We achieve this by providing cutting-edge technology that delivers accurate information in critical moments, reduces administrative burden, and supports evidence-based policing practices.

🔭Our Vision

We envision a future where AI and human judgment work in harmony to create safer communities and more effective, transparent policing.

Our goal is to become the trusted AI partner for law enforcement agencies worldwide, setting the standard for ethical, effective, and innovative technology in public safety.
